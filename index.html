The artist

A bow between two worlds

Achi Bala is an independent violinist whose playing moves between Carnatic, film and world music. His mission: to share Indian culture through Carnatic violin and diverse dance expressions, with an analytical approach that connects traditional beauty with modern audiences.

Years of travel between Houston and Chennai, and a deep love for ballroom dance and Tamil music, shape a voice on the violin that is equally at home in a kutcheri, a concert hall, or a film score.
