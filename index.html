The artist

ChennaiClassical roots
HoustonCultural diversity
AustinCreative bridge

Achi Bala is an independent violinist whose work moves between Carnatic, film, and world music. His performances connect Indian classical vocabulary with modern storytelling, dance, and screen music.

Years of travel between Chennai and Texas, and a deep love for ballroom dance and Tamil music, shape a voice on the violin that is equally at home in a kutcheri, a concert hall, or a film score.

The Waltz of Chennai

The school

Roopa Fine Arts

Roopa Fine Arts is Achi Bala's teaching school for Carnatic violin, Carnatic vocal, and music theory with exam preparation. Established in 2016, built on formal teaching since 2014.

Students take Carnatic music lessons in Austin and Houston in person, and online worldwide. They have completed arangetrams and won prizes in competition.
